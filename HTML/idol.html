Description

Debut

BTS, also known as Bangtan Sonyeondan (방탄소년단) or Bulletproof Boy Scouts, is a South Korean boy band formed by Big Hit Entertainment. They debuted on June 13, 2013, and have since become one of the most popular and influential groups in the world.
